Future

Culture

The Internet has connected societies and their cultures through daily communication. The Internet affects different cultures globally, societies are inspired by each other and culture is modernized faster than ever, the Internet removes the biggest obstacle between us, the geographical obstacle. The Internet is like a big city where artists, writers, designers, programmers, students and ordinary people can learn new skills, buy stuff, and get to know others without having to leave beds. It can be via social media, videos, blogs or online business, instead of you having to travel around the world, the world can come to you.
