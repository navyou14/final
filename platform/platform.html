Internet And Beyond
Navid Karim

Marketing Platform

The Internet enables a whole new way of life, online shopping, online jobs, online marketing, etc. help communities explore new ideas. Today, entrepreneurs can reach a large and varied audience through blogs, social media, videos, podcasts, e-books, newsletters, infographics, etc. Before the Internet, it was not easy for small businesses to reach such a wide audience as today, back then financing was the biggest factor, today creativity and commitment play a much bigger role.
